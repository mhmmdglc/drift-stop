## EN — Short description (≤80)
Hard, punchy quotes that pull you back to your goals. Offline. No account.

## EN — Full description
DriftStop is a personal motivation app that quietly watches your day and sends short, hard-hitting quotes — right when you're about to drift away from what matters.

Not soft. Not toxic positivity. A punch to the chest, not a hug.

• 1000 hand-picked quotes — Stoics, samurai, Rumi, athletes, world proverbs and more
• Smart reminders at random times through your day (you choose how many and the hours)
• Pick your themes — Motivation, Discipline, Focus, Peace, Reckoning, Legacy and more
• A calm home that shows one quote at a time — no endless feed, no doom-scroll
• Revisit what you've seen, or pull a random one from your history
• Favorites, share, and an Android home-screen widget
• Works fully offline. No login. No account. Just quotes and fire.
• Turkish, English, Spanish, German, French, Italian

Made to feel like a worn leather notebook — warm, dark, hand-drawn.

Stop drifting. Get back to work.
